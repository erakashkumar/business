🚀 12-Month Roadmap

Month 1-2: Validate & Anchor

Launch the Gupchup + Projector setup. Focus purely on consistency, establishing the permanent spot, and building the initial local evening crowd.

Month 3-5: Menu Expansion

Once the crowd is locked in, introduce the high-margin items: Steamed Momos and Thick Shakes. This is where the Average Order Value doubles.

Month 6-8: Autopilot Mode

Hire a trusted local operator to handle the 3-hour food serving. I transition to pure management, maintaining the tech and supply chain. Income becomes semi-passive.

Year 1+: The Franchise Blueprint

Use the profits to fund "Smart Cart 2.0" in a different neighborhood. Replicate the exact tech-driven model across the city.
